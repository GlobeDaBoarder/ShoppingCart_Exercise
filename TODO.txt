* ENGLISH *
***********

1. Class diagram
    The file "ClassDiagram.png" and "ClassDiagram_landscape.png" respectively
    represents the structure for your implementation.
    Your tasks is to convert the class diagrams into Java classes and implement its code.
    The behavior is defined through the executable specification - the unit-tests ;-)

    Check your implmentation by means of the give unit test classes
    "ProductTest", "CategoryTest", "CategoryHierarchyTest", and
    "ShoppingCartTest".
